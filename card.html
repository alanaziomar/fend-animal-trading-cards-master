Horse

is one of two extant subspecies of Equus ferus. It is an odd-toed ungulate mammal belonging to the taxonomic family Equidae

  • herbivore and feeds on mainly grasses, herbs, vegetables, and water.
  • prey animal: predators and his best defense is to flee.
  • flight animal: The horse can reach a very high speed when galloping

The simple fact that humans are predators and horses are prey animals indicates that man and horse are very different.
